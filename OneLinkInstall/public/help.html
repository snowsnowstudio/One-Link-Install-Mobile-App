About OnelinkInstall.web.app

OnelinkInstall.web.app is a short-link generator for app downloads.
With OnelinkInstall.web.app you get one short-link to your app no matter if it's on Apple AppStore, Google Play. Instead of having several separate links to each app store, you only need one short link.
Works on iPhone, iPad, Android.

How to use OnelinkInstall.web.app:
OnelinkInstall.web.app is the easy and fuss-free way to link to your app!
Just use link:
https://onelinkinstall.web.app/?ios=id1588812059&android=com.snow.slidepuzzle
and we will determine which to use every time someone is using your OnelinkInstall.web.app short-link.
- id1588812059: ID app/game for iOS
- com.snow.slidepuzzle: Package ID for Android
